VR Military Drone Flying Simulator

About the Project

The Military Drone Flying Simulator is a sophisticated training platform designed for the Indian Army to master drone operations across diverse environments. This simulator addresses the high cost of drone damage during real-world training by offering a virtual alternative. It provides a realistic and immersive environment for trainees to practice drone navigation, mission planning, and tactical maneuvers, ensuring enhanced operational efficiency and mission success.

Key Features

  • Precise simulation of drone controls and flight dynamics
  • Integration with original drone controllers using Arduino for enhanced realism
  • Mission-based training scenarios, including reconnaissance and surveillance
  • Obstacle navigation and precision piloting challenges
  • Multiplayer scenarios for collaborative mission training
  • Real-time analytics for flight performance evaluation

Technologies Used

  • Unity 3D for high-quality simulation development
  • Arduino integration for hardware interaction
  • C# scripting for custom flight mechanics and scenarios
  • Multiplayer integration for team-based training missions
