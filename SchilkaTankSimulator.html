Schilka Tank Simulator

About the Project

The Schilka Tank Driving Simulator is an advanced training tool developed for military personnel to master the operation of Schilka tanks. This simulator provides a highly realistic environment for trainees to practice tank driving, maneuvering, and navigation in diverse terrains and challenging scenarios, ensuring safe and effective training.

Key Features

  • Accurate replication of Schilka tank controls and mechanics
  • Realistic battlefield scenarios with enemy engagement simulations
  • Dynamic terrain handling for off-road navigation
  • Advanced analytics for performance evaluation and improvement

Technologies Used

  • Unreal Engine for high-fidelity simulation development
  • VR integration for immersive training experiences
  • C++ scripting for precise control mechanics
