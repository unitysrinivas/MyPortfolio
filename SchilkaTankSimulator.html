Schilka Tank Driving Simulator

About the Project

There are 97 Schilka tanks in operation across India, primarily used during warfare. The objective of this application is to replicate real-life scenarios and provide an effective training platform for new Indian Army recruits. Training directly with these tanks poses risks, including potential damage to the vehicles and significant operational costs such as transportation. To address these challenges, the Schilka Tank Driving Simulator was developed, offering a cost-effective and risk-free solution to train recruits while preserving the operational readiness of the tanks.

Key Features

  • Accurate replication of Schilka tank controls and mechanics
  • Dynamic terrain handling for off-road navigation
  • Hardware integration using Arduino Mega with sensors like proxy, potentiometer, and push buttons
  • Hydraulic 3-DOF motion platform to replicate real-world tank movements

Technologies Used

  • Unity Engine for simulation development
  • C# programming for custom simulation logic
  • C++ programming for Arduino hardware integration
  • Hydraulic motion system for immersive motion replication
