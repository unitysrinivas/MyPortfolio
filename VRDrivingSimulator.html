VR Driving Simulator

About the Project

The VR Driving Simulator is a virtual reality-based training tool crafted to train new Indian Army recruits efficiently in a safe and controlled environment. It offers 8 distinct vehicle variants, complete with realistic terrains to mimic real-life scenarios. This simulator also incorporates dynamic weather conditions and real-time traffic systems, providing a comprehensive and immersive training experience while ensuring operational safety. By utilizing this simulator, training becomes risk-free and cost-effective, eliminating the challenges associated with real-world training.

Key Features

  • Realistic vehicle physics with customizable parameters
  • Dynamic weather effects and terrain simulations
  • Interactive tutorials and training scenarios
  • Performance tracking and analytics for skill assessment
  • Supports 8 distinct vehicle variants for diverse driving experiences
  • Real-time traffic systems to simulate realistic driving challenges

Technologies Used

  • Unity 3D for simulation development
  • Meta SDK for VR integration
  • C# scripting for custom mechanics
